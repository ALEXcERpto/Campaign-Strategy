Deep Market Research

Enhanced Research Prompt
You are a B2B market research analyst conducting comprehensive market intelligence for [CLIENT_COMPANY]. Develop an exhaustive market analysis that covers:
 
**COMPANY ANALYSIS:**
- Core service offerings and delivery methodologies
- Revenue models and pricing strategies  
- Geographic presence and market penetration
- Operational capabilities and team structure
- Technology stack and infrastructure
 
**MARKET LANDSCAPE:**
- Total Addressable Market (TAM) size and growth trends
- Market segments currently served vs. untapped opportunities
- Buyer journey and decision-making processes
- Seasonal trends and cyclical patterns
- Regulatory environment and compliance requirements
 
**VALUE PROPOSITION ANALYSIS:**
- Primary value drivers for different customer segments
- Quantifiable ROI and business impact metrics
- Competitive advantages and market positioning
- Pricing power and value perception in market
 
**COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE:**
- Direct competitors: offerings, pricing, market share
- Indirect competitors and alternative solutions
- Competitive gaps and white space opportunities
- Win/loss factors in competitive situations
 
**GO-TO-MARKET CHALLENGES:**
- Sales cycle complexity and typical friction points
- Buyer education requirements and market maturity
- Channel conflicts and partner dynamics
- Economic sensitivity and budget allocation patterns
 
**OUTPUT FORMAT:**
Provide a structured analysis with quantifiable insights, specific industry metrics, and actionable intelligence that will inform targeting and messaging strategies.
Quality Control Checklist
Quantifiable market size data included
Competitive landscape mapped with specific companies
Clear value propositions identified
Market challenges specifically outlined
Industry-specific terminology captured
